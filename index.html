Why This Exists

I have recognized the lack of learning resources for high school and elementary students in the Philippines. This contains links, files, lessons, and study guides to help students learn ICT.

There are also speacial features in this site. For example, when I give assignments to my students, I give them special code to answer keys once the assignments have been checked so they could check it when they want to review.

However, I do not aim this to be an exclusive repository. Rather, I intend this for public use. For more information on which content from here are allowed for your use, you can check out the license.
